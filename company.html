who we are

With 70 years of experience, Secco Sistemi is the leading Italian brand in the production of integrated systems for doors, windows and shutters, and façades in galvanized steel, stainless steel, corten or weathering steel and brass. Every year it transforms 2 million metres of section bars in 200,000 doors and windows. Fixtures are interpreted as part of an integrated and versatile system at the architect’s disposal, the results blending industrial technology with creativity and craftsmanship. 

Secco Sistemi guarantees maximum levels of sustainability, comfort and wellbeing.

The company collaborates with important professional experts and leading figures on the contemporary scene of architecture, the common denominator being the passion for materials, attention to detail and the culture of the project.

certifications

The constant research that Secco Sistemi is involved in – oriented towards improving the processes of realization of the products and output of services furnished – is made systematic by means of the following:

  • the use of a company quality system ISO 9001 (verified by the German certification body TÜV)
  • the partnership with the German institute of testing and certification IFT Rosenheim, which makes possible – thanks to SECCO LAB and its authorized laboratory for official tests – the research, experimentation, inspection and certification of each new product. 

sustainability

For Secco Sistemi, sustainability means investing in research to aim at maximum efficiency in reduction of energy consumption; responsible use of natural long-lasting, recyclable and re-usable materials; inspections and controls in all the phases, from conception of the idea to production and finally disposal.
