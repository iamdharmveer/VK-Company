materials

The metal alloys used by Secco Sistemi are of excellent quality and have precise characteristics: they are natural, integral and safe, ductile and versatile.

Besides being appreciated for their structural resilience and durability over time despite requiring low maintenance, these materials are also recyclable and re-usable.

Galvanized steel, stainless steel, corten steel and brass, with their varying surfaces and finishes, present extraordinary multiple potential solutions for interpreting the design project in the construction phase, blending into the various different settings and landscapes proposed by the range of environments, climates, tradition and culture. The expertise and knowledge of the properties of these precious materials have led us, in collaboration with the architects, designers and engineers right from the preliminary phases, to identify the best solutions for using the selected material.

Moreover, in the production phase, building with Secco Sistemi means being able to rely on assistance and technical support with all the elements of the 12 product lines at clients’ disposal, in the 4 materials with their varying finishes.
